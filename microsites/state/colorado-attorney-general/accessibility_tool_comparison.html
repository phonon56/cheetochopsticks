What automated tools can and cannot catch — industry consensus

The 30–40% rule: Every automated accessibility tool — Siteimprove, WAVE, axe, Lighthouse — detects roughly the same 30–40% of WCAG issues. The remaining 60–70% require human judgment, keyboard testing, or screen reader verification. Siteimprove was active on every coag.gov page and still missed all 95 findings in this audit. The audit method matters more than the tool.
