What happens next

1 Intake team reviews your submission within 3–5 business days and routes it to the right unit.
2 AI-assisted classification assigns the complaint to Consumer Fraud, Civil Rights, Environmental, or another section.
3 If you provided contact info and follow-up is warranted, a representative will contact you.
4 Anonymous complaints are investigated but cannot receive direct responses.

Individual legal representation, advice on your specific case, or guaranteed outcomes.
