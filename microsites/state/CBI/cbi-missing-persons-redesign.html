Missing Person Contact Form

The CBI Missing Persons Unit uses this form to keep contact information current for family, friends, and associates of missing persons in Colorado. Your information helps us reach you for case-related communication and event invitations.

This form is not for reporting a missing person. If you need to report a loved one as missing, please call your local law enforcement agency immediately, or call 303-239-4201 for the CBI 24-hour line.
