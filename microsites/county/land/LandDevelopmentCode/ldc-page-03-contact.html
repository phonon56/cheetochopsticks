Official Record
Submit Written Comment for Hearings

To submit written comment that enters the official administrative record for Planning Commission or BOCC hearings, use the hearings email. Written comments carry the same legal weight as speaking in person and must be submitted at least 24 hours before the scheduled hearing.

✉
Hearings Email
PCDHearings@ElPasoCO.com
Required in every hearing comment
  • File number or project name (for LDC: reference the specific module or section)
  • Your full name and mailing address
  • Your position: support, oppose, or neutral with conditions
  • The specific criteria you're addressing (master plan reference, code section)
  • Submit at least 24 hours before the hearing date

Direct Representation
Contact Your Commissioner

For the LDC adoption vote, your most direct leverage point is your elected commissioner. Contact them before the vote — not after. Two sentences and your address is enough. They read constituent contact.

🏛
Find your commissioner
bocc.elpasoco.com ↗
Enter your address to find your district and commissioner's direct contact.
What to say in two sentences
  • State your name, address, and that you're a constituent in their district
  • Reference a specific LDC section or standard you care about
  • State what you want them to do: require X, strengthen Y, clarify Z
  • Specific asks are more actionable than general support or opposition
