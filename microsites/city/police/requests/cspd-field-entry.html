The premise Let's help them do the job they were hired to do. Form fatigue is real and prevalent in the most capable professionals. Officers are hired to respond, investigate, and write fair reports — forms should not be their primary concern all the time. The driveraction null rate and the impossible dates aren't about discipline. They're what happens when a desk-designed form meets a roadside reality. Defaults, picklists, and validation at the point of entry carry the weight the officer shouldn't have to.
