What is this form?

Use this form to request records held by the City of Colorado Springs. You do not need to know a document number or RFP number. Describe what you are looking for in plain language and we will route your request to the right department.

This form is accessible on any device and works with screen readers and keyboard navigation. If you need assistance, see the alternative contact information at the bottom of this page.

Do not include sensitive information in this form. Do not send credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, or passwords. Payment for records is collected separately by Accounts Receivable.

Submit by email instead

The online form will prepare your request and open your email client with everything pre-filled. Review and send from there. Your email client must be set up on this device for this to work — if it is not, use the phone or mail options below.

What happens after you submit

Day 0
You submit. You receive an email confirmation with a reference number. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or call (719) 385-5906.
Day 1–3
City staff review your request. If records are available, they may be provided immediately. If more than two hours of staff time is needed, you receive a cost estimate before any charges are incurred. You may confirm, modify, or withdraw at that point.
Up to Day 10
If extenuating circumstances apply, the City may extend by up to seven additional working days. You will be notified in writing before Day 3.
If denied
You have the right to a written explanation citing the specific statutory exemption under C.R.S. § 24-72-204. You may also contact the Colorado Freedom of Information Coalition for assistance.

By submitting you acknowledge fees may apply for staff time beyond two hours. You will be notified before any charges are incurred and may withdraw at that time.
