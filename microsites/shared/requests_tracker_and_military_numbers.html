The published workforce numbers (60,892) include civilian employees and contractors who live off-post and DO appear in census data. Active duty only — the figure that creates housing demand distinct from civilian demand — is not broken out in the published totals. That is what the FOIA seeks. For now, Fort Carson's 2020 Census on-post count of 17,693 is the only verified on-post residential population figure.
