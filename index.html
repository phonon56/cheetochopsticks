What is this, exactly?

cheetochopsticks is a civic data project built by two residents of Colorado Springs — StorySeed Studios and The Dais — at a kitchen table, with public records, too much coffee, and a sincere belief that people who live somewhere deserve to actually understand it.

Everything here is free. No paywall, no pitch deck, no monetization strategy. No invoice attached. If it helps one person ask a better question at a city council meeting, or understand why their street feels dangerous, or find out which metro district is charging them a mill levy they can't look up — that's the whole point.

The Alerts button in the nav is real. Those nine notifications are current. If you want to know when something new drops — before the press release, before the tweet — bookmark this page and check the ticker. We'll be here.
