What is this, exactly?

cheetochopsticks is a civic data project built by two residents of Colorado Springs — StorySeed Studios and The Dais — at a kitchen table, with public records, too much coffee, and a sincere belief that people who live somewhere deserve to actually understand it.

Everything here is free. No paywall, no pitch deck, no monetization strategy. No invoice attached. If it helps one person ask a better question at a city council meeting, or understand why their street feels dangerous, that's the whole point.

If you want to know when something new drops — before the press release, before the tweet, before the newsletter — we're working on a way to do that. For now, bookmark this page and check back when you remember. We'll be here, sawdust and all.
